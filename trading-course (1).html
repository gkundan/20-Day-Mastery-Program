SEE THE BOSS

Learn to read every candle like a story — Day 1 to 5

Day 1–5
DAY 01
What is a Candle?
▼
📖 Story

Every candle = a battle between buyers ⚔️ and sellers. One side wins. The candle shows you who.

👁 Visual — Green vs Red Candle
🟢 GREEN
Buyers Won
🔴 RED
Sellers Won
📌 Parts of a candle:
Body = open to close price range
Upper wick = how high price went
Lower wick = how low price went
Green: price closed ABOVE open
Red: price closed BELOW open
🎓 Learn
Green candle = buyers pushed price up — buyers WIN
Red candle = sellers pushed price down — sellers WIN
🎮 Practice
Open any chart (TradingView / Zerodha Kite)
Count 20 candles — how many green? How many red?
DAY 02
Strong vs Weak Candle
▼
📖 Story

Big candle = strong warrior 💪 charges forward with power.
Small candle = tired warrior 😴 — confused, indecisive.

👁 Visual — Strong vs Weak Candles
STRONG
Big body
Power!
WEAK
Small body
Confusion
STRONG
Big body
Attack!
DOJI
No winner
Pause!
Big body = lots of movement = strong conviction
Small body = little movement = no clear winner
Doji = open ≈ close = total confusion
Tip: Big body candles matter most. Small ones = be careful
🎮 Practice
Open a chart — mark 5 candles with BIG bodies
Now mark 5 candles with SMALL bodies (doji-like)
Notice: big body candles often continue, small ones often reverse
DAY 03
Wick = Rejection
▼
📖 Story

Wick = "attack failed!" Price tried to go somewhere but got PUSHED BACK by the opposite side. A long wick = strong rejection.

👁 Visual — Wick Types & What They Mean
Long UPPER Wick
Sellers rejected
buyers at top
Long LOWER Wick
Buyers rejected
sellers at bottom
Shooting Star
Very bearish
strong rejection
Hammer 🔨
Very bullish
strong rejection
Long upper wick = sellers pushed price DOWN hard
Long lower wick = buyers pushed price UP hard
The LONGER the wick, the STRONGER the rejection
Shooting star near a high = danger! Price may fall
Hammer near a low = opportunity! Price may rise
🎮 Practice
Find 3 candles with long UPPER wicks — what happened after?
Find 3 candles with long LOWER wicks — what happened after?
Find a Shooting Star or Hammer pattern
DAY 04
Close Power
▼
📖 Story

WHERE a candle closes tells you who is truly in control. Closing near the HIGH means buyers are still STRONG. Closing near the LOW means sellers are still STRONG.

👁 Visual — Close Position = Power Level
Close near HIGH
💪 Very bullish
Buyers dominate
Close at MID
Neutral
No clear winner
Close near LOW
💪 Very bearish
Sellers dominate
Close at top of range = buyers were strong ALL day
Close at bottom of range = sellers were strong ALL day
Close in middle = fight is equal, be careful
🎮 Practice
Find 5 candles that closed near their HIGH
Find 5 candles that closed near their LOW
Did the next candle continue in the same direction?
DAY 05
Combine Candle Psychology
▼
📖 Story — Read a Chart Like a Movie

Now combine everything: size + wick + close position. Each candle is a sentence. Together they tell a story.

👁 Visual — A Mini Story (5 candles)
READ THIS STORY LEFT → RIGHT
① Big red candle — Sellers ATTACK hard, close near low
② Small red candle — Sellers still going, but losing energy
③ Doji — Nobody wins, everyone is resting
④ Small green — Buyers try to push up, but body is small = weak
⑤ Big red again — Sellers come back STRONGER, buyers give up
🎮 Practice
Pick any 5-candle section on a real chart
Write a story: "First the buyers attacked... then sellers..."
Do this 3 times on different sections

TREND

Identify who controls the market — Day 6 to 8

Day 6–8
DAY 06
Trend Basics
▼
📖 Story

The market is always walking in a direction 🚶 — up, down, or sideways. Your job is to SEE the direction, then follow it. Never fight it.

👁 Visual — Uptrend vs Downtrend vs Sideways
⬆ UPTREND — Higher Highs & Higher Lows
⬇ DOWNTREND — Lower Highs & Lower Lows
🎓 Learn
Uptrend = price makes HIGHER highs + HIGHER lows (staircase up)
Downtrend = price makes LOWER highs + LOWER lows (staircase down)
Sideways = no clear pattern, prices go back and forth
🎮 Practice
Open 5 different charts and identify: Up / Down / Sideways?
Mark the Higher Highs and Higher Lows on one uptrend
DAY 07
Who is Boss?
▼
📖 Story

In an uptrend, BUYERS are the boss. In a downtrend, SELLERS are the boss. Always trade WITH the boss. The boss usually wins.

🎓 Simple Rule
In uptrend → only look for BUY opportunities
In downtrend → only look for SELL opportunities
In sideways → avoid trading (no clear boss)
🎮 Practice
For 5 charts: who is the boss? Buyers or sellers?
Write it down: "On this chart, sellers are boss because..."
DAY 08
Don't Fight the Boss
▼
👁 Visual — Right vs Wrong Trade Direction
DOWNTREND — Only sell. Never buy against it.
🚫 Golden Rules
Don't BUY in a downtrend — fighting the boss
Don't SELL in an uptrend — fighting the boss
Don't trade in sideways — no boss, no direction
🎮 Practice
Find 3 charts where someone might buy against a downtrend
See what happened: did the price fall anyway?

PATTERN

Impulse → Pullback → Break — Day 9 to 12

Day 9–12
DAY 09
Impulse
▼
📖 Story

Impulse = a strong ATTACK. Multiple big candles all moving in one direction. This is the trend showing its power. It moves FAST.

👁 Visual — Bullish Impulse Move
IMPULSE — Big candles, all green, moving up fast
All candles are BIG
All candles close near their HIGH
Price moves a LOT in a short time
⚠️ Don't CHASE an impulse — it's already moving. Wait for pullback.
🎮 Practice
Find and mark 5 impulse moves on charts
They can be up (bullish) or down (bearish)
DAY 10
Pullback
▼
📖 Story

After the impulse attack, the warriors REST 😴 — price moves back a little in the OPPOSITE direction. This is NOT a reversal. It's a breather. Smart traders WAIT for this.

👁 Visual — Impulse then Pullback
PULLBACK — small opposite candles after big move
Pullback = small candles, going opposite to trend
Pullback is where you WAIT to enter
⚠️ If pullback candles become BIG, maybe the trend reversed — be careful
🎮 Practice
After each impulse you found yesterday, find the pullback
Are the pullback candles small? Good. Big? Be careful.
DAY 11
Break (Continuation)
▼
📖 Story

After the rest, the trend attacks AGAIN. A strong candle appears at the end of the pullback, breaking in the trend direction. THIS is your entry signal!

🎓 Entry Signal
A strong candle (big body, close near high/low) appears
It breaks in the direction of the main trend
This confirms the pullback is over — trend continues!
🎮 Practice
Mark continuation candles that appear after pullbacks
Check: is the candle big? Does it close in the trend direction?
DAY 12
Combine the Full Pattern
▼
👁 Visual — Full Pattern: Impulse → Pullback → Break
THE COMPLETE SETUP — This is what you look for every day
🎮 Practice
Find the FULL pattern (Impulse + Pullback + Break) on a real chart
Mark all 3 stages with different colors
Find 3 examples of this pattern

MARKET BEHAVIOR

Traps, confusion zones, and real moves — Day 13 to 15

Day 13–15
DAY 13
Trap
▼
📖 Story

The market tricks emotional traders 😈 — it makes a fast, sudden move to lure them in, then REVERSES. The trap candle looks exciting but it's a fake.

👁 Visual — Bull Trap (fake breakout, then drop)
TRAP — Price spikes up, emotional traders buy, then it falls
Fast spike with no buildup = DANGER sign
If price breaks resistance but has no trend behind it = trap
Patient traders: wait to see if the move is REAL, don't chase
DAY 14
Confusion Zone
▼
👁 Visual — Confusion Zone (Sideways / Choppy)
CONFUSION — Small candles, no direction = NO TRADE
🎓 Learn
Small mixed candles going nowhere = confusion zone
This is where amateurs lose money over-trading
Pro rule: If you can't clearly see who is boss → don't trade!
DAY 15
Real Move
▼
📖 Story

After confusion, the winner is decided. A big candle breaks out of the range with FORCE — this is the real move. Patient traders who waited are now rewarded.

🎮 Practice
Find a confusion zone (small choppy candles) on a chart
Identify what came before it — was there a trap move?
Find the REAL move that broke out of the confusion

FULL SYSTEM

Build your complete trading approach — Day 16 to 18

Day 16–18
DAY 16
System Build
▼
📖 Your System

Trend + Pullback + Strong Candle = Entry. All 3 must be present. If even ONE is missing — skip the trade.

🎓 3 Conditions Checklist
① Trend is CLEARLY up or down (not sideways)
② A pullback has happened (small opposite candles)
③ A STRONG candle appears confirming trend continues
DAY 17
Entry Practice
▼
🎓 Enter ONLY Here
At the END of the pullback
When a strong candle appears in trend direction
Never enter in the MIDDLE of an impulse
Never enter without a trend being clear
🎮 Practice
Find 3 valid entry points on historical charts — all 3 conditions must be met
DAY 18
Avoid Mistakes
▼
🚫 3 Big Mistakes
Late entry — price already moved, you missed it
Middle entry — entering during impulse, not pullback end
No trend — entering when market is sideways/confused
