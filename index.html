Day 01
The Market is a War
▼
Story

Forget everything. The market is a war between BUYERS and SELLERS. One side wins, the other loses. Your job? Watch who is winning, then pick a side and trade with them. That's it.

The Concept

When price goes UP, buyers are in control. When price goes DOWN, sellers are in control. When it moves sideways, they are tied. Every single candle shows you who just won that round.

Key Insight

You are not trading price. You are trading trapped traders. A trapped trader is forced to exit = creates movement = your profit comes from their pain.

Day 02
Candle Anatomy
▼
Story

Every candle has 4 prices: OPEN (where it started), HIGH (peak), LOW (bottom), CLOSE (where it ended). Together they tell you exactly what happened in that timeframe.

The 4 Parts of a Candle
Green Candle
Buyers Won
(Close > Open)
Red Candle
Sellers Won
(Close < Open)
Practice
Find any 5 GREEN candles. For each one, say: "Buyers bought, pushed up, and held the high"
Find any 5 RED candles. For each one, say: "Sellers sold, pushed down, and held the low"
Day 03
Wicks Tell the Truth
▼
Story

The thin lines (wicks) at the top or bottom of a candle show REJECTION. Long upper wick = sellers rejected buyers. Long lower wick = buyers rejected sellers. The longer the wick, the stronger the rejection.

Wick = Rejection Signal
Long Upper Wick
Sellers slapped
price back down
Long Lower Wick
Buyers pushed
price back up
Practice
Find 3 candles with long UPPER wicks — what happened after?
Find 3 candles with long LOWER wicks — what happened after?
Day 04

Day 09
What is a Pullback?
▼
Story

In an uptrend, price goes up... then takes a small rest backwards... then continues up. That rest = pullback. It's the boss taking a breath before the next push.

How to See It

Trend is UP → Price drops (pullback) → Price resumes UP. Same for downtrends: DOWN → bounces up → continues DOWN. Pullbacks are temporary moves AGAINST the main trend.

Day 10
Pullback Entry Rules
▼
The Setup

UPTREND → PULLBACK DOWN (2-4 candles) → STOP (pullback ends) → STRONG CANDLE UP (confirmation) → ENTRY.

What To Look For
Trend was clear and strong
Price pulls back 20-40% of the previous move
Pullback lasts 2-4 candles maximum
STRONG candle closes the pullback (big, near high)
Day 11
Where to Exit?
▼
Story

STOP LOSS = right below the pullback low. TARGET = at least 1.5x the distance you risk. If you risk 10, you must make 15+.

Risk:Reward Rules
Risk = Entry to Stop Loss
Reward = Entry to Target
ONLY trade if Reward ≥ 1.5 × Risk
Never take 1:1 — bad risk management

Where the price MUST go next
Day 12
Day 12
Find Liquidity Levels
▼
Story

Liquidity = pockets of EQUAL HIGHS or EQUAL LOWS. Price loves these levels because traders have stops bunched there. Smart traders hunt these zones to force exits = create movement.

Where to Look
Equal Highs (price touched same level 2-3 times)
Equal Lows (price bounced off same level 2-3 times)
Round Numbers (like 50000, 40000, 30000)
Previous Swing Highs/Lows
KEY INSIGHT

In UPTREND, price must break ABOVE equal highs = liquidity is above. In DOWNTREND, price must break BELOW equal lows = liquidity is below. This is where your target should be.

Day 15
What is a Trap?
▼
CRITICAL CONCEPT

A TRAP = price breakout that looks strong but IMMEDIATELY REVERSES. Traders who bought the breakout = TRAPPED = forced to exit = creates violent movement = YOUR PROFIT.

How Traps Work
Price breaks out ABOVE resistance (looks bullish)
Retail traders buy the breakout
Smart money was waiting here (with stops)
Sudden reversal STOPS trapped traders
They exit = violent sell-off = DOWN move
The Phrase

👉 "If breakout = TOO FAST + IMMEDIATE REVERSAL = TRAP"

Trap Signs
✕ Huge candle breaks above resistance
✕ Very high volume on breakout
✕ But then NEXT candle reverses hard
✕ Closes back below the breakout level
The Opportunity

When you see a trap, the smart move is OPPOSITE of the breakout. If breakout was UP then reversal = trade DOWN. If breakout was DOWN then reversal = trade UP.

Day 16
Liquidity Sweep & Stop Hunt
▼
Story

Smart traders know that most people put stops JUST BELOW the lows. They hunt these stops = create a spike through the low = catch stops = then reverse. This is called a LIQUIDITY SWEEP or STOP HUNT CANDLE.

The Pattern
Price is in uptrend, bouncing off support
Suddenly ONE candle spikes DOWN (long lower wick)
This spike STOPS trapped shorts (they exit)
Next candle REVERSES UP hard
Price continues the uptrend
TRAP CANDLE RULE

A stop hunt candle = long wick against the trend + immediate reversal. This is a STRONG signal that the trap caught shorts = uptrend continues.

Practice
Find 2 false breakouts (trap candles)
Find 2 stop hunt candles (spikes that reverse)
Mark what happened AFTER each trap
